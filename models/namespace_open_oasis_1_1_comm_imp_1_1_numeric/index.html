@Desc : Abstract solver class to provide unified interfaces. Solver's responsible for parsing the equation expression to be solved, and discretizing the equation items in the computational domain, combining them into a matrix, and solving it.

Solver is also responsible for initializing the relevant field quantities, and providing specific discrete and stepping scheme.
